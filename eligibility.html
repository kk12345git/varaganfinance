Requirements

Who Can Apply?

At Varagan Finance, we believe in inclusive growth. Our eligibility criteria are designed to be transparent and accessible while maintaining our premium standard of financial security.

Age Criteria

Minimum age of 21 years at the time of application and maximum 65 years at loan maturity.

Employment Status

Salried professionals, self-employed individuals, and business owners with a stable income source.

Residency

Must be a resident of Tamil Nadu with valid address proof and identity documentation.

Minimum Income

Monthly net income requirement varies by loan type, starting from ₹15,000 for Personal Loans.
