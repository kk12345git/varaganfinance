Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At Varagan Finance Pvt Ltd, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

We collect information that you provide directly to us when applying for a loan or contacting us, including:

Your data is used solely for the purpose of:

We implement strict security measures to ensure your data is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
